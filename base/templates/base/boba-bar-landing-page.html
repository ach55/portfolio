Our Story

Hsin-Yi Chou grew up in Taipei, Taiwan. From a young age, Hsin-Yi was captivated by the art of tea since her parents owned a small tea shop where she spent countless hours watching her parents brew tea. The shop became a community hub, people gathered to relax and enjoy a moment of peace amidst the busy city life. As Hsin-Yi grew older, she studied culinary arts in Taipei, her dream was to open a relaxing space where people could experience the true essence of tea, like her parents did. After completing her studies, Hsin-Yi moved to Halifax, Nova Scotia, a city known for its diverse and welcoming community. She saw an opportunity to share her Taiwanese heritage by introducing a new concept in bubble tea culture.

Our Values

Quality: At The Boba Bar, we are committed to delivering the highest quality tea. From the sourcing to the use of fresh ingredients, we ensure that every drink meets the highest of standards.

Innovation: Our menu features unique creations you have never tasted before and our goal is to give customers a one-of-a-kind experience.

Our Teas

At The Boba Bar, we love to push the boundaries of tea by experimenting with new and exciting flavors. By incorporating a splash of your favorite alcoholic beverages, we create unique fusions that marry the rich, comforting essence of tea with the vibrant, invigorating flavors of classic cocktails and mocktails. Whether you're in the mood for a twist on a timeless drink or eager to try something entirely new, our carefully crafted concoctions promise a delightful journey that'ss as refreshing as it is comforting.
